Chimichurri Backed Chicken

Chimichurri is a dish from Argentina that is usually served over grilled chicken or steak. This ia a version which may or may not be authenic.

Ingredients

  1. 1/2 Cup finely Chopped parsley
  2. 2 1/2 table spoons olive
  3. 2 tablespoons chopped fresh oregano leaves
  4. 2 cloves garlic, minced
  5. 1/4 teaspoon
  6. 1/8 cup teaspoon red pepper flakes
  7. 1 pinch freshly ground black pepper
  8. 1(3 pound) whole chicken
  9. 1 teaspoon oliveoil
  10. 1 large onion
  11. 1/2 cup chicken broth
